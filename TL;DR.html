  • TL;DR

Sineks Golden Circle is a model that explains how great leaders inspire action by focusing on three key elements: Why, How, and What. The "Why" represents the core belief or purpose that drives an individual or organization. The "How" outlines the process or values that set them apart from others. The "What" refers to the tangible products or services they offer. By starting with "Why," leaders can create a sense of purpose and connection that resonates with others, leading to greater loyalty and engagement. Click here for a longer summary of our report
